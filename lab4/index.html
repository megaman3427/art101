Task 1: How to Bake a Cake

    Ingredients
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cup all purpose flour
  • 1 3/4 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 cup milk
    Directions
  1. Preheat oven to 350℉
  2. Grease/butter your cake pan
  3. Mix all your wet ingredients together
  4. Mix all your dry ingredients in a separate bowl
  5. Add your dry ingredients to your wet ingredients and stir in the milk
  6. Pour your batter into the cake pan you prepared
  7. Bake for 30-40 minutes
  8. Cool completely before serving
